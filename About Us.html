About Simba Construction Company

Our Mission

At Simba Construction Company, our mission is to provide high-quality construction services that exceed our clients' expectations. We strive to deliver projects on time and within budget, ensuring the highest standards of craftsmanship and safety.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Quality: We are committed to excellence in every project we undertake.
  • Safety: We prioritize the health and safety of our workers and clients.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and techniques to improve our services.

Our History

Founded in 2000, Simba Construction Company has grown from a small local contractor into a leading construction firm. Over the years, we have successfully completed numerous residential and commercial projects, building a reputation for quality and reliability.

  • Comercial Construction

  • Project management
